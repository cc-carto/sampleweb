Cold Snaps, Scarce Chargers: The Winter Reality of EV owners

In Lab 1 of Geog 572, we were tasked to create an ArcGIS Story Map of a topic of our choice. I decided to cover the story on Electric Vehicles (EVs) that were failing in the cold winters of Chicago. The story highlights the issues that EVs face from a lack of charging facilities and temperature drops, as well as the solutions that people have come up with to combat this problem and what the future looks like for the EV industry and market.
