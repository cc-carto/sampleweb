Wisconsin: Best Public Golf Courses

The first lab of Geog 370 was to create a state map which included 5 points of interest, with the theme being of our choice. I thought doing something golf related was very fitting as it is a sport that I really enjoy doing. The idea also stemmed from playing collegiate golf for the Women's Golf Team at Wisconsin and it was only after I moved here did I realize how many amazing golf courses the state of Wisconsin had to offer, with many of them being open to public play.
